Corn Cake

Make a delicious corn cake using a blender

The corn cake is one of the most traditional delicacies served during the Junine festivities, and it is also considered one of the iconic dishes from Northeast of Brazil. Here we will use canned corn and corn flakes, but you can use corn cobs grated instead.

That is one of the main reasons I selected this dish, and you will be impressed how easy and fast is to make it. This corn cake is humid, fluffy, delicious, and it is perfect for your breakfast, afternoon tea, or for a picnic.

Ingredients:

  • 1 canned corn
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup corn flakes
  • Â½ cup oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • Butter (for grease the pan)
  • All purpose flour (for flour the pan)

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 210C.
  2. Grease and flour a rectangular medium-size cake pan.
  3. Add to a blender the canned corn, milk, sugar, corn flakes, oil, and eggs, and mix it for around 3 minutes.
  4. Add the baking powder to the blender, and mix with a spoon or use the pulse a few times.
  5. Add the cake mixture to the pan and bake it for 40 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Let stand until it is warm. Unmold the cake, and cut in square format. It is ready to serve.
